Tokyo Jihen

Tokyo Jihen or Tokyo Incidents (東京事変 Tōkyō Jihen) was a Japanese rock band formed by Ringo Sheena, after leaving her solo career. The band's debut single "Gunjō Biyori" was released in September 2004, and they ended activities in February 2012. The band sold 2,3 million copies.
