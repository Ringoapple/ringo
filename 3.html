5th album

Hi Izuru Tokoro (日出処, "The Land of the Rising Sun"), also known as Sunny [1], is a fifth studio album by Japanese musician Ringo Sheena released on November 5, 2014 by Universal Music Japan sublabel Virgin Records. It is a compilation studio album compiling singles released since 2009 as well as new compositions
